Who we are:

EURODEMY has started its journey from Kuwait, with being specialized in providing all various training services in different fields, including law, management, media, leadership, self-development, finance, and accounting, as well as other fields related to a person's, both male and female, professional and social life in Kuwait and the other Arab countries. We, at EURODEMY, highly believe in that everyone has their own talents and capabilities, and it is our responsibility to search for and develop these talents and capabilities, in the interest of the individual and society. Based on this, our importance has come from the valuable solutions we provide to the problems and challenges facing the individuals within different organizations, work environment, and family.

Since its start, EURODEMY has succeeded to provide many great solutions to many clients of different ages and places with achieving its clients' satisfaction with all the services and solutions provided. Therefore, we have high rates of registration for our training programs.

Mission

Since our start, we have had a noble mission and we have been working hard to achieve it. This mission is to develop all employees in Kuwait and the other Arab countries for participating in the development process occurring in the Arab region.

Vision

We are looking forward to being on the top of training and its relevant consultation services industry all over the Arab region, and to being the primary destination to all Training and consulting, personally and professionally, seekers.

Goals

1

Achieving quality, uniqueness, and innovation in the field of training and its relevant consultation services.

2

Continuously improving our services for meeting our Arab society's expectations and developments.

3

Constructive participation in our society development process and effective engagement with the Arab countries' development visions.

4

Developing and improving our trainees' skills and abilities through providing training programs, with following the best modern and international standards.

5

Increasing the number of our branches to cover as many cities as possible inside and outside the country.

Values

Believing in that training is our only strategic way to communicate and keep up with the future.

Transparency

The center devotes all its capabilities and expertise to develop its clients' skills.

Quality

Quality is one of the topmost values we are seeking to achieving within our center and into our clients.

Seriousness

We take all the training programs seriously regarding choosing the material, selecting the academically and scientifically qualified and experienced experts and consultants for achieving the highest level of seriousness and effectiveness.

Follow-up

We follow up our trainees and their response to our training programs, with identifying and enforcing strengths and identifying and overcoming weaknesses.

Communication

We are looking forward to achieving communication within the entire educational process, as it is a great value must be existing within our society in whole.
